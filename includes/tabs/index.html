About Hamro Tour

mission

Mission of Hamro Tour is to attract more tourists to the country. It is set to tap the outbound travelers of the neighboring nations to Nepal in the context of rising number of outbound travelers in India and China.

vision

Vision of Hamro Tour is to bring in two million tourists each year by the targeted year. Kathmandu will be fist busy places, fewa lake Pokhara will be 2nd, Lumbini and Chitwan will be 3rd busy places in tourist arriving destinations.

passion

Hamro Tour has registered young tourism entrepreneurs and enthusiastic trekking and travel agency. The government has commited to provide the excellent services to the tourists coming from different countries of the world.
